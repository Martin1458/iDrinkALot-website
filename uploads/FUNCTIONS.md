# iDrinkALot — Feature Copy for Promotional Material

One entry per feature. Each has a title, a short description (1–2 sentences), and bullet points.
Add a screenshot next to each one.

---

## Watch your curve in real time.
Log a drink, see the line move. The Widmark formula plus your body stats and stomach state, recalculated every second.
- Sober-by time updates as you drink
- Empty / Full stomach slider
- Promille ‰ or BAC %, your call

---

## Every drink, timestamped.
Add anything from the catalog in two taps. Forgot to log one earlier? Dial back the time and drop it in where it belongs.
- 100+ drinks across beer, wine, shots, and cocktails
- ABV and volume adjustable per drink
- Backdate up to 4 hours with the time picker

---

## Set your limits before you start.
Goals for units, beers, shots, wines, and cocktails — pre-loaded at the start of every session. Hit your limit and get a nudge. Or don't set one at all.
- Per-category limits (beer / shot / wine / cocktail)
- Optional water break reminder when you hit your goal
- Resets automatically each session

---

## Know your week before Friday.
A live running total of your units against whatever limit you've set. No end-of-week surprises.
- Weekly unit progress bar on the home screen
- Turns red when you go over
- Set your own limit in Profile

---

## Drink with friends, track together.
Start a session and invite your friends in the same move. Everyone sees each other's drink count, units, and BAC estimate — live.
- Invites sent as push notifications
- Join from the in-app banner with one tap
- Tap any member to see their full BAC graph and drink timeline

---

## Share your session after.
When you end a session, turn it into a polished stats card and share it anywhere — or post it straight to the social feed so your friends can see it.
- One-tap export as a clean image (works with Instagram, WhatsApp, iMessage)
- Group session card shows every member's totals side by side
- Add a caption before you post

---

## Attach a photo to your session.
A night out has more to it than a BAC curve. Attach a photo when you end your session and it stays linked to that session forever.
- Shown at the top when you open any past session in your history
- Appears alongside your stats on the public feed
- Picked from your camera roll, cropped to landscape

---

## Your friends' feed, for real.
See what your friends are drinking — in real time, in your feed. Like a post, leave the night where it belongs.
- Scrollable activity feed sorted by most recent
- Like any session post with a tap
- Photos, captions, and stats all in one card

---

## Add friends by QR or code.
No usernames to guess. Scan someone's QR code or swap the 8-character code and you're connected.
- Every account gets a permanent, unique friend code
- QR code generates instantly — show it, scan it, done
- Share your code as a link from any messaging app

---

## See how your friends drink.
Tap any friend in your list to pull up their all-time records — biggest day, favourite drink, total sessions, and more.
- Total drinks, units, and sessions at a glance
- Biggest day, most drinks in a day, average per session
- Favourite drink highlighted

---

## Pick your venue, or let it find you.
Type the name of the bar, pick from nearby places, or just use a custom label. The venue shows up on your session, your share card, and your history.
- Location-based nearby bar suggestions
- Type any venue name freehand
- Venue saved to session and visible to group members

---

## Your history, no filter.
See exactly what your drinking looks like — by day, by week, by drink type. The data doesn't moralize.
- Units by day bar chart
- Average units by day of week
- Top 5 most-logged drinks
- Calendar heatmap of drinking days

---

## Tap any session to replay it.
Every session in your history is expandable. Open one and see the full BAC curve, every drink you logged, and the exact times.
- Full BAC graph recalculated from your body stats at the time
- Full drink list with timestamps
- Works on solo and group sessions

---

## Records worth knowing about.
Biggest day ever. Longest sober streak. Favourite drink. All-time totals. The kind of numbers you can share at the pub or keep to yourself.
- Biggest session units and drink count
- Drink streak and sober streak
- All-time totals and per-session averages
- Total likes your sessions have received

---

## The Drinkdex.
A running log of every drink you've tried from the catalog. How much of it have you worked through?
- Tracks your first time trying each drink
- Moon phase progress bar (🌑 → 🏆)
- Unlocks automatically as you log new drinks

---

## Search 600+ cocktails by name.
Can't remember the ABV of a Jungle Bird? Look it up, log it, and it lands in your session with the right numbers already filled in.
- Powered by CocktailDB
- Auto-calculates units from the recipe
- Saved to your personal catalog for next time

---

## Works offline.
Log drinks underground, in a basement bar, or anywhere with bad signal. Everything syncs quietly when you're back on.
- Drinks and sessions saved locally first
- Auto-syncs in the background on reconnect
- No spinner, no waiting, no lost entries
