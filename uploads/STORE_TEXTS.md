## Description

iDrinkALot is a drinking tracker. Not a wellness app. Not a habit-change journey. A drinking tracker.

Log drinks as you go and watch your BAC curve update live, powered by the Widmark formula with a real absorption model built around your weight, sex, age, and how much you ate. The app does the maths. You do the drinking.

WHAT IT DOES
- Live BAC curve and sober-by time, updated as you drink
- Stomach fullness slider that actually changes your curve
- 300+ drinks to log, ABV and volume adjustable per drink
- Backdate a drink up to 4 hours if you forgot
- Weekly unit tracker that goes red when you go over your limit
- Session goals per drink type (beer, shot, wine, cocktail)

DRINK WITH FRIENDS
Start a shared session and invite your friends before the night begins. Everyone sees each other's drink count, units, and live BAC estimate in real time. Tap any member to pull up their full graph.

THE SOCIAL FEED
Post your session when the night ends. Your friends see it in their feed with photos, your stats, and a like button. Tap any post to open the full breakdown. Add up to 4 photos. Tap any friend to see their all-time records. No usernames, no social graph mining — just QR codes and 8-character friend codes.

LOOK BACK
Full session history with BAC curve replays, weekly charts, a calendar heatmap, all-time records, and a Drinkdex tracking every drink you've ever tried. Because of course.

No ads. No moralising. BAC calculations are estimates. Don't drive.
