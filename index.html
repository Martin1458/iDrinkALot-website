Track the night,
not the morning after.

A pocket-sized drink tracker for nights out. Log what you drink, watch your BAC live, and see the whole table's stats in real time.

Want to help test it? Drop your email and we'll ping you when open testing starts.

No spam. One email when testing opens, that's it.

01, Live BAC

Watch your curve in real time.

Log a drink, see the line move. Widmark formula plus your body stats and stomach state, recalculated every second.

  • Sober-by time updates as you sip
  • Empty / Full stomach slider tunes the curve
  • Promille ‰ or BAC %, your call
02, Catalog

Tap. Logged. Done.

Beer, shots, wine, cocktails. Add anything in two taps, or backdate one you forgot to log earlier.

  • 284 drinks so far, volume adjustable, and track calories
  • Backdate up to 4 hours with the time picker
  • "Tried" badges on everything you've sampled
03, Drinkdex

How many have you tried?

Every drink in the catalog tracked against your history. Watch your percentage tick up as you work your way through it.

  • 284 drinks, one progress bar
  • Updates automatically as you log new drinks
  • "Tried" badges carry across every session
Stats → Drinkdex
04, Sessions

One screen, the whole night.

Units, calories, duration, peak BAC, sober-by time. Plus your goals, ticking down as you go.

  • Pin a location so you remember where it happened
  • Goal bars for beers, shots, wines, cocktails
  • Drink timeline you can scroll back through
05, Limits

Set it before you start.

Weekly unit cap and per-category session goals, pre-loaded every time. Hit the limit, get a water-break nudge. Or skip it.

  • Weekly units, plus beer / shot / wine / cocktail caps
  • Optional water-break popup on goal hit
  • Resets automatically each session
Profile → Limits
06, Group sessions

The whole table, in sync.

Start a session, ping your friends, and watch each other's drinks roll in live. Tap anyone to see their full BAC curve and timeline.

  • Pick a bar by location, invite friends in one tap
  • Live drink count, units, and BAC for every member
  • Leading BAC, most drinks, most units, called out automatically
07, Friends

Add by code. Or by QR.

Friend codes for typing, QR for face-to-face. Tap any friend to see their lifetime records — total units, biggest day, favourite drink.

  • Eight-character codes, or scan a QR with the camera
  • Lifetime stats per friend, on tap
  • No feed, no public profile, no algorithm
08, Friend records

What's their biggest day?

Total drinks, total units, sessions, biggest day, average per session, favourite drink. Pull up the receipts on any friend.

  • Lifetime totals across every session
  • Biggest day and most-drinks-in-a-day records
  • Favourite drink, with how many times they've ordered it
09, Records

Numbers worth knowing.

Biggest day. Longest sober streak. Total units logged. The kind of stats you can share at the pub or keep to yourself.

  • Records, charts, and timeline views
  • Filter by 7d, 30d, 3m, or all-time
  • Drink streaks and sober streaks, side by side
10, Breakdowns

Your week, in shapes.

By category, by day of week, top drinks. The data doesn't moralize — it just shows up.

  • Units by category with percentages
  • Average units per day of week
  • Top 5 most-logged drinks, all-time
Stats → Charts
